There are more places than just Op de Kop to do acro-stuff at. We'll
(try to) keep the list below updated as well as possible.

If you know of or organize acro-stuff and want to be added to this list: please
contact us. If you see incorrect or outdated information here: please let us
know.

## Regular classes, open trainings or jams in or nearby Utrecht

- Every Monday: acroyoga/acrobatics open training at [Hart van Hoograven (organized by Zina and Andrey)](https://chat.whatsapp.com/Hj2Ybi8oR3948l9qxWazzG)
- Every Friday: acroyoga classes by [Playful People Tribe](https://www.playfulpeopletribe.com/classes)
- Every Thursday: acroyoga classes at [Yoga Moves](https://yogamoves.nl/styles/acroyoga/)
- Various circus classes for kids at [Diedom](https://diedom.nl/)
